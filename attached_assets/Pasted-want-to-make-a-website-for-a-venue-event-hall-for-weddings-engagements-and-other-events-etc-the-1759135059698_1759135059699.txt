Occasions Marquee – Brochure Website (Frontend Only)

Purpose
Create a fast, elegant, and mobile-responsive website for Occasions Marquee that serves as an online brochure. The goal is to showcase the venue, highlight services, display photos of past events, and provide a simple way for visitors to get in touch.

No backend or CMS will be used — all content will be hardcoded/static. Inquiries will be handled by a third-party form service (e.g. Formspree or Netlify Forms).


Objectives
Build trust and establish a premium brand image.
Encourage potential clients to reach out via the inquiry form.
Present venue information clearly with strong visuals.
Keep development, hosting, and maintenance simple & low-cost.

Scope & Features
Homepage
Hero slideshow with high-quality marquee images.

Tagline (e.g., “Where Your Occasions Become Memories”).

Call-to-action buttons: “Book a Tour”, “View Gallery”.

About Section

Short description of Occasions Marquee.

Key differentiators (capacity, ambiance, central location).

Venue Spaces / Services

Info on hall capacity, décor, lighting, seating arrangements.

Optional: package highlights (without exact pricing).

Gallery

Image grid (categorized: weddings, receptions, engagements).

Lightbox view for larger previews.

Lazy loading for speed.

Testimonials

3–5 quotes from clients (static text).

FAQs
Simple accordion with common questions (capacity, food policy, etc.).
Contact / Inquiry Form
Name, email, phone, event date, guest count, message.
Uses Formspree/Netlify → sends directly to admin email.
Google Maps embed with venue location.
Footer
Contact info (phone, email, address).
Social media links (Instagram, Facebook).
Quick links to main pages.

Homepage: dynamically loaded hero slider, key CTAs.

Responsive Navigation: menu that collapses on mobile, sticky header, smooth scroll to sections.

Gallery / Portfolio: filterable / categorized gallery (e.g. by event type).

Inquiry Form: fields such as event date, event type, expected guests, contact info, message. USE 3rd party liek formspree or netlify forms


Social Integration: embed Instagram feed (e.g. via Instagram API or plugin), Facebook page link.

Map & Contact: embed Google Maps (or equivalent), address block, phone clickable, email link.

Performance Optimization: lazy loading images, caching, compress images, CDN (if needed).
